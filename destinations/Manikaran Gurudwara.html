MANIKARAN GURUDWARA

Manikaran is located in the Parvati Valley on river Parvati, northeast of Bhuntar in the Kullu . Traditional feeding (Lunger) of visitors at Sikh gurudwara at Manikaran, Himachal Pradesh.Tucked at an altitude of 1760 m above sea level, Manikaran enjoys winters and low temperatures all throughout the year. The summer season, starting from April and lasting till June-end is considered the best time to visit Manikaran as the climatic conditions are favourable for the tourists.
