RIVER RAFTING

River rafting in Manali is a gripping water sport that awakens the adventure enthusiast in you. River Beas and its tributaries promise to offer an exciting river rafting experience in Manali for every adventure buff. In addition to river rafting, the Beas River in Manali offers canoeing and kayaking. White water rafting in Manali generally starts from Pirdi or Mohal and continues until Jhiri. Manali is one of the most appealing tourist destinations in Himachal Pradesh. Adventure sports play an important role in enticing tourists from all over the globe to Manali. However, river rafting in Manali is among the most popular adventure sports. Manali opens arms to adventure enthusiasts from all across the world with various adventure sports.
