DYE IT YOURSELF

Dye It Yourself (DIY) which is a state of the art, one of a kind workshop where participants can learn the art of dyeing with natural colors. DiY aims is to educate and create awareness among participants about use of natural dyes and its positive impact on environment. During this activity we try and use waste from our kitchen & surrounding which can be helpful for natural dyeing. The idea for the workshop came from the fact that chemical dyes are one of the major sources of pollutants today in textile. Our aim is for people to include sustainability as a filter before any purchase of textiles.
