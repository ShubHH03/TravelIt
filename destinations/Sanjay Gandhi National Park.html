Sanjay Gandhi National Park

The Sanjay Gandhi National Park, also known as the Borivali National Park, is one of the few open-air parks in Mumbai. The landscape background and the clean environment make the park the perfect place for outdoor activities. At the park visitors have plenty of activities to indulge in. One could choose to go on a trek, embark on a long scenic walk to the historic Kanheri Caves (you can even spot a few wild animals if you’re lucky). Do carry your camera, for there are plenty of opportunities for a photograph.
