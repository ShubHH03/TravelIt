ICE SKATING

India's largest and only all-weather indoor ice skating rink. Spread over an area of 15000 sq. ft., ISKATE gives you the opportunity to experience skating on real ice. Anyone who likes roller skating will have a natural knack for ice skating. This is a perfect escape from the blazing heat of Delhi. An interactive penguin guide will make sure that your kids have fun and learn the activity.
