Kukas Air balloon

Drift over the Pink City in a hot air balloon and experience the mesmerizing views of the forts and palaces nestled among the Aravalli ranges with Sky Waltz Balloon Safari in Kukas. Enjoy the plethora of colors and sounds of this city from up above. Hot-air balloon ride is one of the popular things to do at the adventure places in Jaipur for one day and are mostly held few hours before the sunrise and sunset, and each ride can accommodate up to 8 people.
