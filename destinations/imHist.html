INDIAN MUSEUM

Regarded as one of the oldest museums in Asia, the Indian Museum was established back in 1814 and called Calcutta Indian Museum. To catch a glimpse of the illustrious history of Kolkata as a city and West Bengal as a state, you might want to opt for a visit here. The main reason why the museum is also recognised as a monument in Kolkata is because of its heritage status as an old museum. From Gandhara art to antiques from the Mohenjo Daro and Harappa civilizations, there are plenty of excellent exhibitions and antique collections that would certainly marvel you at the Indian Museum
