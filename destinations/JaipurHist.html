JAL MAHAL

This palace impresses its visitors with the fact that it is situated in the center of Lake Sagar. The peaceful and splendid surroundings of the palace are something that will keep you tempting to visit the place again and again. With a magnificent view, this palace is one of the most visited places in Jaipur. The architecture of the palace will welcome you with some breath-taking views.

Best time to visit


Between October - January
11 am - 10.30 pm
INR 10 Indians, INR 50 foreigner

JAIGARH FORT

This fort is very popularly known as ‘victory fort’ and is located above the Amber Fort. Standing on the top of this fort, you can get the best view of the Aravalli Hills. This fort has some wonderful palaces, museums, temples, and gardens which are sufficient enough for attracting the tourists. The architecture of the fort also helps in giving a distinguished identity to the fort and also in making this historic palace quite popular.

Best time to visit


Between November - February
9 am - 4 pm
INR 15 Indians, INR 100 foreigners

Popular Food Places


1.Cho -Vietnamese Kitchen & Bar
2.Olive Bar & Kitchen
3.Bo Tai Qutub



BIRLA MANDIR

It won’t be wrong to say that this temple is one of the most beautiful temples of Jaipur which has been successful in attracting the tourists and visitors since its construction. It is a beautiful, splendid and peaceful pilgrimage site for the Hindu devotees. Located at Moti Dungari Hill’s base, this temple reflects the amazing Jaipur’s architecture with its white marble design.

Best time to visit


Between October - March
6 AM-12 PM 3 PM-9 PM
INR 150 per person
